About Me

I was born in Athens, Greece, in 1985. I received a Diploma in Mechanical Engineering in 2009 and an MSc in Automation Systems in 2011 from NTU Athens. I'm a PhD Candidate in School of Chemical Engineering in NTUA, in the area of Model Predictive Control for Nonlinear Systems. My academic interests include Model Predictive Control, Automation Systems, Robotics, Embedded Systems, Python Programming, Optimization, Artificial Intelligence etc. In the past I worked as Mechanical Engineer in food industry, as a web developer for various companies and as research engineer in research projects in NTUA. Since 2016 I have been working in space industry for a greek company as a software engineer, writing software for space missions.
